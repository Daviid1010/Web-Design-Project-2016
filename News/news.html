Munster Narrowly Beaten Away By Tigers in Thrilling Game

Munster this weekend lost their second leg match against the Tigers in what most would agree was a thrilling game. Only one try scored in the game by Munster's hooker Nial Scannel near the end of the match. Leicester's Owen Williams scored a penalty from the half way line in the last second to win the match for the Tigers. Great performance from both sides overall dispite a few soft penalties given away by both teams.


Leicester Tigers Suffer Heavy Defeat at the hands of Superb Munster

Munster ran four tries past Leicester in Thommond Park in what was Munster's best game this season. Munster were very well disciplined defensively conceding no points and scoring 38 against their opponents. Leicester on the other hand suffered from very poor discipline giving away many penalties. Great to see this kind of preformance from a Munster side.
